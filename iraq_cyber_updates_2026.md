# Iraq Cybersecurity Posture Updates (2024-2026)

## Latest Rankings (as of Dec 2025/Jan 2026)
- **National Cyber Security Index (NCSI):** 115th (Score: 24.17) - *Significant improvement from previous score of 10.00.*
- **Global Cybersecurity Index (GCI) 2024:** Tier 4 (Score: 53.07) - *Up from 20.71 in 2020.*
- **E-Government Development Index (EGDI):** 148th (Score: 45.72)
- **AI Readiness Index:** 107th (Score: 40.91)

## Key Strategic Developments
- **National Cybersecurity Strategy:** Approved in December 2022. The strategy is now being implemented with support from international partners like EUAM Iraq.
- **National Development Plan 2024-2028:** Includes strategic objectives for digital transformation and AI integration.
- **Iraqi National Framework for Secure AI Adoption:** Introduced in late 2025 to guide the safe use of artificial intelligence.
- **Cybersecurity Policy Fulfillment (NCSI):** 20% (3/15). Iraq now has a recognized National Cybersecurity Strategy (Indicator 1.4).

## Infrastructure & Technical Progress
- **Digital Enablers (NCSI):** 17% (2/12).
- **Education & Professional Development (NCSI):** 50% (5/10). This remains a relative strength.
- **Cybercrime Legislation:** Ongoing efforts to strengthen the legal framework against cybercrime, identified as a major challenge for 2025-2026.

## Ongoing Challenges
- **Cybercrime:** Identified as "Iraq's Next Big Challenge" in late 2025.
- **Critical Infrastructure Protection:** Still scoring 0% on NCSI for CII protection.
- **High-level Leadership:** Lack of a single, centralized high-level cybersecurity authority remains a gap in the NCSI assessment.
- **Geopolitical Risks:** Iraq remains a "soft target" in regional cyber conflicts.
