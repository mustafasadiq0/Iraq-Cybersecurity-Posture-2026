## Key Strategic Developments and Initiatives

Iraq has undertaken several strategic initiatives to bolster its cybersecurity posture:

*   **National Cybersecurity Strategy (NCS):** Approved in **December 2022**, this strategy serves as a foundational document for guiding national cybersecurity efforts. Its implementation is ongoing, with support from international partners such as EUAM Iraq, which facilitates strategic exchanges and capacity building.
*   **National Development Plan 2024-2028:** This comprehensive plan integrates strategic objectives for digital transformation and the secure adoption of artificial intelligence across various sectors, demonstrating a forward-looking approach to national development.
*   **Iraqi National Framework for Secure AI Adoption:** Introduced in **late 2025**, this framework aims to provide guidelines and best practices for the safe and ethical integration of AI technologies, addressing potential security risks associated with emerging technologies.
*   **Cybercrime Legislation:** Efforts are underway to strengthen the legal framework against cybercrime, recognizing it as a growing threat. This includes developing and enforcing laws that align with international standards to combat various forms of cyber offenses.

These developments signify a growing awareness and commitment within Iraq to address cybersecurity challenges at a national level. However, the implementation and enforcement of these strategies and frameworks will be crucial for tangible improvements in the country's overall cybersecurity resilience.
